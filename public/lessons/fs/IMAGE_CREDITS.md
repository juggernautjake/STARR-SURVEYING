# FS prep — image credits & commercial-use licensing

Tracks every image used in the FS/SIT prep modules, its source and license, and
whether it is **cleared for commercial use** or **flagged for replacement**
before commercial distribution. Keep this current whenever an image is added.

Legend:
- ✅ **CLEAR** — Starr-owned original OR public domain. No restriction.
- 🟡 **ATTRIB** — Creative Commons requiring attribution (BY). Fine commercially *with credit*.
- 🟠 **REVIEW (share-alike)** — CC BY-SA. Usable, but derivatives must stay CC BY-SA; some commercial uses prefer to replace. **Candidate for swap.**
- 🔴 **REPLACE** — copyrighted / unknown license. Personal use only; **must be replaced for commercial use.**
