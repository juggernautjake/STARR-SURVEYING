## Pricing

Jewels are magically enhanced precious gems that can be added to items such as amulets, armor, or weapons or can be held on their own.

Cost of a jewel

One enhancement - 100 Solidas

Two enhancements - 250 Solidas

## Item Slots

Jewels have such a strong resonance that they will become inert if positioned too closely to another jewel. The magic of the jewels can also prove to be overwhelming for people, and the use of more than five jewels at a time results in the bearer of the magic falling unconscious. The standard way that jewels are worn is described below.
- Head (1 slot available)
- Arms (1 slot - even if the item only takes up one arm)
- Legs (1 slot - even if the item only takes up one leg)
- Torso (2 slots available)

## Eldritch Jewel Enchantments

Eldritch jewels can be enchanted to retain magical powers. They generally take a single action to activate and can be recharged if their power is spent.

Enchanting jewels

The initial process of enchanting an Eldritch Jewel requires a DC 30 spellcraft check and requires an inherently magical component. If the character does not make the DC, then the work is wasted. Normally items such as dragon scales, phoenix feathers, or other items from magical creatures are used. These items generally cost 1 solidas and are destroyed when the check is made, regardless of success or failure.

On occasion, many small precious gems are used in place of a large one for the purposes of enchanting. This increases the DC by 5 for each enchantment. The total weight of gems required for the standard enchantment is 0.2 grams.

It takes 5 working days, at 10 hours a day, to produce a single Eldritch Jewel with one enhancement. The process begins again and new magical components are required every time the crafter adds a new enchantment.

Burnt out jewels

A DC-20 Arcana check is required to recharge a burnt out item. If the character does not make the DC, they take a number of points of nonlethal damage equal to the difference between their result and the DC. The item is fully charged regardless of the result of the check.

The character must be trained in the Arcana skill to attempt this check. This activity takes three actions.
