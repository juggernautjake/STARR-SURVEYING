## General Feats

Advanced Magic
- Description Requires training in Spellcraft and to have learned a single spell that has an Advanced spell ability.Learn an Advanced spell. Every time you cast this spell, make a DC 20 Will save or lose an action on your next turn.

Alert
- Description Requires training in Perception. You are never considered to be flat-footed so long as you can see 5 feet of your surroundings.

Arcane Heritage
- Description Requires training in Arcane. Either you or an ancestor was exposed to powerful magic of a certain type, and after this exposure, you have learned to master that type of magic. Choose one benefit from the list below.
- Aligned Choose one of the abilities listed below based on the cataclysmic magical event that impacted you or your family. Entropy Change the relevant ability score for one skill of your choice, and change the ASB that you get from your class upon character creation. All abilities gained from that class are now dependent on the new ability score instead of the old one. You can only change mental stats to other mental stats or physical stats to other physical stats with this ability. Order Gain a +2 bonus on all of the skills from your background. Vice The duration of all harmful effects that you apply to other creatures increases by 1 round. Some examples include bleed damage, a condition from Divine Blessing, or a penalty from a spell. Instantaneous effects are not impacted by this. Virtue Whenever you use the support action, you gain advantage on the die roll.
- Draconic Gain two draconic features from the list below ClawsGain two natural attacks that each deal 1d6 points of slashing damage. Elemental Resistance Resist an amount of elemental damage equal to your level each round. This only applies to one element, which you choose upon taking this feat. Scales Gain DR 2, which stacks with all other forms of DR. Wings Gain a fly speed of 10 feet.
- Elemental You gain a ranged elemental attack that you are proficient with, akin to the Wizard’s Arcane Blast. You can only use one type of elemental damage, selected when you get this feat. This attack has a range of 30 feet and deals damage equal to your level + your Charisma modifier.
- Fey You learn to cast a single spell of your choice from the enchantment school using your arcane skill and your charisma modifier instead of spellcraft and your intelligence modifier.
- Nature Gain the ability to create a natural storm, as the power from the Druid subclass. Use your Arcane skill and your charisma modifier instead of Nature and your wisdom modifier.
- Occult Gain a life-draining melee attack that deals an amount of damage equal to your charisma modifier. This damage is force damage and you heal an amount equal to your charisma modifier if the attack is successful.
- Time Take two points of nonlethal damage to gain an extra action this turn.
- Vampiric Gain a life-draining bite attack that deals 1d6 piercing damage and 1d6 bleed damage. If a creature has an active bleed effect, you may spend an action to drain their blood, increasing the bleed damage by two that round and gaining HP equal to the total damage. The creature may make a reflex save opposed by your bite attack to avoid this.

Archivist
- Description Requires training in Spellcraft and the ability to cast at least one spell from the Spell List. Learn how to cast two spells from one school. The school must be in a school that you already know a spell from. You can never learn the Advanced or Expert versions of any of these spells. Special: If you use a skill other than Spellcraft for your spellcasting, you can select this feat. You still must be proficient in Spellcraft.

- Description Requires training in Religion.Make a religion check as an action. A number of allies within 30 feet of your character when you make the check (with a maximum number equal to your Wisdom modifier), can use your result on a check this round. You may only use this ability on one type of check (Chosen when you select this feat) and you must be trained in the check to use this ability. The three types of checks that this ability applies to are listed below.Skills SavesAttacks (only with proficient weapons) If a character has Aura Mastery and Divine Blessing, the range of Divine Blessing increases from melee to the same range as Aura Mastery and can affect the same number of targets as Aura Mastery.
- Advanced You may select this feat an additional time to use it on another type of check.Eg: the first time a character gains this feat they choose skill checks. This would allow them to make a Religion check and allow their allies to use their total on any one skill check they roll over the next round. The character then selects this feat again and chooses saves. The next time they are in combat, the character chooses to prepare a Will save for the other players using this ability.

Bomb Crafter
- Description Requires training in Craft: Bomb. You learn how to make two kinds of bombs from the list below. You can make a number of bombs per day equal to your Craft: Bomb skill. You must acquire the materials for your bombs. Materials needed for each bomb are listed with each of them and are used up after the bomb explodes. The bomb casing can be made with almost any kind of metal and is also used up upon explosion. A bomb counts as a unique weapon which you gain proficiency with upon taking this feat. It has a range of 20 feet.
- Bright Bomb Creatures within sight must make reflex saves against your Craft: Bomb check or else be blinded for a number of rounds equal to your intelligence modifier. Duration: InstantaneousMaterial: Firefly.
- Explosive Bomb Deals 1d6 + your intelligence modifier in fire damage to target creature. All creatures within 1 hex of the bomb are treated as if attacked by the bomb and must make a reflex save to negate the attack. Duration: Instantaneous.Material: Ammonium nitrate.
- Gunk Bomb All creatures within one hex must make a reflex save against your Craft: Bomb check. Failure results in entanglement. The gunk cannot be removed unless the creature takes fire damage or washes it off. Duration: Lasts for a number of rounds equal to the crafter’s Craft: Bomb skill. Material: Thick oil.
- Lightning Bomb Deals 1d6 + int modifier in electricity damage to target creature. Reflex negates. Duration: InstantaneousMaterial: Bottled lightning.
- Smoke Bomb Covers a 5-foot radius when used and expands 1 square each round while active. Duration: Lasts for a number of rounds equal to the crafter’s Craft: Bomb skill. Material Required: Incense.
- Poison Bomb Deals 1d6 Str or Con damage to all creatures adjacent to the hex it was thrown into, and any creatures in that hex. The creatures may make a save as if they had inhaled the poison, with a -2 penalty for being engulfed by the bomb. Duration: InstantaneousMaterial Inhaled poison.

Eldritch Smith
- Description Requires Spellcraft and Craft: Eldritch JewelsYou gain the ability to manufacture Eldritch Jewels. The initial process of enchanting an Eldritch Jewel requires a DC 30 spellcraft check and requires an inherently magical component. If the character does not make the DC, then the work is wasted. Normally items such as dragon scales, phoenix feathers, or other items from magical creatures are used. These items generally cost 1 solidas and are destroyed when the check is made, regardless of success or failure.On occasion, many small precious gems are used in place of a large one for the purposes of enchanting. This increases the DC by 5 for each enchantment. The total weight of gems required for the standard enchantment is 0.2 grams.It takes 5 working days, at 10 hours a day, to produce a single Eldritch Jewel with one enchantment. The process begins again and new magical components are required every time the crafter adds a new enchantment.Eldritch Sand can be created from Eldritch Jewels. This requires a DC 20 Craft: Eldritch Jewel check and produces 100 grains from a standard Jewel. This process takes 2 working days. Grains cannot be recharged after used, as they are absorbed into the substance of whatever they enchant.
