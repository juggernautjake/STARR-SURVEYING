### Powers
- **Aspect:** Select one aspect for your companion creature. A creature can only have one aspect; once it is chosen, it cannot be changed.

If a creature has an Intelligence modifier that is no more than +1 higher than yours, you may use the ability below. Make a Diplomacy or Arcane check opposed by your target’s will save. - **Paired Strikes:** As an action, you and your companion creature can each make a strike against a target within reach of both of you. MSP applies to these strikes as normal. Your creature makes this strike as a free action.

### Specialization
- **Extraordinary Creature:** The creature gains two ASBs to any scores and one ASB to intelligence or wisdom.

### Greater Specialization
- **Extraordinary Creature:** The creature gains two ASBs to any scores and one ASB to intelligence or wisdom.
- **Supernatural Creature:** The creature gains access to a single spell from the Wizard list which it can cast. The creature gains a +2 bonus on Arcane checks.

- **Final Aspect:** You gain one of the Aspects that your companion can choose. Additionally, your companion gains two of the benefits listed below. 10 skill ranks to apply to any number of skills. 20 bonus HP A static +5 damage bonus on all attacks. 4 Ability Score Increases.

# Conduit

### Material List
- **Fluids:** Water in all of its forms and other liquids that are not components or byproducts of living creatures.
- **Fine Particles:** Dirt, sand, or any other material if it has been ground into fine pieces.
- **Gems:** Diamonds, rubies, etc.
- **Metals:** All metallic substances, either worked or unworked.
- **Organic Matter:** Material from living organisms and their byproducts. You are not able to transmute these materials if they are still alive or are attached to a living being.
- **Oozes:** Cooking oils, crude oil, and other viscous liquids.
- **Stone:** All types of rock including coal, granite, shale, and more.
