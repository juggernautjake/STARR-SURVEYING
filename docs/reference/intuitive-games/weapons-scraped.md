## Melee Weapons

Standard weapons are categorized into groups based on size, classification, and damage type. A character gains proficiency with a specific Damage Type in a Weapon Classification. The three damage types are Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing.EG: A character may be proficient with a Heavy Slashing weapon or a One-Handed Piercing weapon.

### Melee Weapon Classifications

Classifications determine the cost, amount of damage that the weapon does, and impact proficiency.
- Light - 10 Solidas Light weapons always have the throwing property in addition to any other property they may have,
- One-Handed - 15 Solidas Item description

- Heavy - 30 Solidas Item description

### Melee Weapon Properties

Properties are the special abilities of a weapon. All melee weapons have one of the properties listed below.
- Alternate Damage (Add 1 Solidas to the cost) Add an additional damage type to your weapon. The weapon only deals one damage type at a time. You may switch between these damage types as a free action.
- Double-Ended (Add 10 Solidas to the cost) Your weapon requires you to use both of your hands and deals damage on each end. The damage amount is the same on each end, but the sides do not need to match in damage type.
- Expanded Critical You can achieve a critical success while attacking with this weapon by totalling 15 more than your opponent instead of 20.
- Nonlethal This can only apply to bludgeoning weapons. The weapon now deals nonlethal damage.
- Powerful Critical Your critical successes with this weapon deal triple damage instead of double damage.
- Throwing The weapon now has the option to be thrown, dealing damage as normal. The range for a thrown weapon is 20 feet. Light Weapons automatically have the Throwing property. These weapons may choose the Greater Throwing property to have a thrown range of 50 feet.
- Reach (Add 5 Solidas to the cost) You can attack targets who are 10 feet away from you while wielding this weapon.
- Engineered Can only be used by a character proficient with the weapon group.Allows the character to make checks with a single combat skill as if they were trained in it as long as they are using that weapon to make the check.

## Ranged Weapons

Ranged weapons are categorized into groups based on size, classification, and damage type. A character gains proficiency with a specific Damage Type in a Weapon Classification. EG: A character may be proficient with a Heavy Slashing weapon or a One-Handed Piercing weapon.

### Weapon Classifications

Classifications determine the amount of damage that the weapon does and impact proficiency.
- One-Handed Ranged - 10 Solidas These weapons deal 1d6 points of damage and do not deal additional damage for the wielder’s strength modifier.Range 50 feet
- Two-Handed Ranged - 20 Solidas These weapons deal 1d8 points of damage and do not add any additional damage for the wielder’s strength modifier.Range 80 feet
- Heavy Ranged - 30 Solidas A Heavy Ranged weapon deals additional damage equal to the Strength requirement associated with it.Normally these weapons are a Compound Bow or Crossbow, both of which require the wielder to meet the Strength requirement to reload the ammunition.Range 80 feet
- Ammunition - 1 Solidas Most ammunition costs 1 solidas for 50 units of ammunition.

### Ranged Weapon Properties

Properties are the special abilities of a weapon. All melee weapons have one of the properties listed below.
- Additional Range Multiply the range of your weapon by 1.5
- Expanded Critical You can achieve a critical success while attacking with this weapon by totalling 15 more than your opponent instead of 20.
- Powerful Critical Your critical successes with this weapon deal triple damage instead of double damage.
- Engineered Can only be used by a character proficient with the weapon group.Allows the character to make checks with a single combat skill as if they were trained in it as long as they are using that weapon to make the check.

## Special Weapons

Special weapons are any Melee or Ranged weapon that has more than one Property. For each additional property, the weapon decreases in damage dice.
