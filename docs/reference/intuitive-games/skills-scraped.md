## General Skills
- **Tumble:** The character may move around another character swiftly, not allowing their opponent to make an attack of opportunity against them. This is accomplished with a stride action, and their Acrobatics check is opposed by the opponent’s reflex save.
- **Leap:** The character may attempt to jump horizontally, vertically, or some combination of both. The standard DC for such an action is 5 per 5 feet of distance, but certain variables are included below. This movement provokes reactions. If jumping vertically upward, double the DC. If jumping with a running start, reduce the DC by 5. A running start requires 10+ feet of movement before the jump. If jumping from wet conditions, increase the DC by 5. If landing on a wet surface, you must exceed the DC by 5 or else fall prone after completing the check.
- **Balance:** If in narrow, wet, or slick conditions that may cause you to lose your balance, you may make a balance check as a free action during your movement. The standard DC for such a check is a 15, and there are variables listed below that may impact the check. Heavy Winds: +5 to DC Hidden Hazards: +5 to DC These could include roots underneath ice.
