# Backgrounds

### Academic

To be an academic means that you have been exposed to higher learning, though you may not have been at the top of your class. You have learned esoteric arts alongside your formal studies. Because of this intellectually stimulating environment, you develop a keen intellect or a strong personality. Some people have both.

Starting HP: 8

Ability Score Boost to Charisma or Intelligence.

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Arcane
- Lore
- Linguistics
- Religion
- Defensive Stance You gain disadvantage on all attack rolls, and advantage on all Reflex saves.
- Advanced Stance Starting at Level 5, you gain Damage Reduction equal to half your level.

### Acolyte

To be an acolyte means that you have been raised as an active member of a pious community. While in this community, you have learned facts about the material world, how to interact with whatever lies beyond it, and how to interact with fellow members of your community. In this environment, you developed perseverance in mind and body, or at least in one of the two.

Starting HP: 10

Ability Score Boost to Constitution or Wisdom.

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Diplomacy
- Lore
- Religion
- Mobile Stance The character no longer provokes reactions from moving through a threatened area
- Advanced Stance Starting at level 5, you no longer provoke reactions.

### Artist

To be an artist means that you have pursued your passions before other things, seeing your projects as more important than whatever the world has in store. In your pursuit of these passions, you pick up an odd assortment of talents that range in practical value. You develop some combination of precision and social grace as you vault between projects.

Starting HP: 8

Ability Score Boost to Charisma or Dexterity.

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Artistry
- Craft
- Lore
- Profession
- Supportive Stance You always count as flanking when threatening an enemy if there is an ally who also threatens the enemy.
- Advanced Stance Starting at level 5, you grant flanking allies an attack bonus equal to half your level.

### Cosmopolitan

To be a cosmopolitan requires that you live in a bustling area, likely a city, port, or some other place that is recognizable on a map. In this fast-paced environment, you acquire obscure knowledge and learn how to navigate your business without disrupting others. As you are exposed to a wide array of travelers, you work hard days exposed to the elements or else acquire an inquisitive mind as you learn from diverse traditions.

Starting HP: 10

Ability Score Boost to Constitution or Intelligence.

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Lore
- Profession
- Stealth
- Swarming Stance You gain advantage on attack rolls when flanking.
- Advanced Stance Starting at Level 5, you gain a bonus on attack rolls equal to half your level when flanking.

### Hunter

To be a hunter means that you have lived your life in the wild. Even if you have a steady, traditional home, your time is spent with the flora and fauna. You learn how to navigate the untamed world with the many hours you spend there, and have had to assert yourself over the beasts with a quick wit or a strong body.

Starting HP: 10

Ability Score Boost to Strength or Wisdom.

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Handle Animal
- Nature
- Perception
- Offensive Stance You gain advantage on all attack rolls, but disadvantage on all Reflex saves.
- Advanced Stance Starting at level 5, you gain a bonus on all damage rolls equal to half your level.

### Laborer

To be a laborer is to be the farmhand, the sea dog, or to practice innumerable other professions requiring a strong back. You learn your trade and your body is powerful, if not beautiful, from the endless work.

Starting HP: 12

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Craft
- Profession
- Neutral Stance You ignore all of your opponents’ stance bonuses that enhance attack rolls, including flanking effects.
- Advanced Stance Starting at Level 5, you ignore all of your opponents’ stance bonuses.

### Merchant

To be a merchant, your business must come first. Your proficiency with wheeling and dealing matters as much as your ability to manufacture your products, so you learn all of it. If your personality isn’t loud enough to get ahead, you’ll need to have the most efficient and precise service possible to keep the customers coming.

Starting HP: 10

Ability Score Boost to Charisma or Dexterity.

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Bluff
- Craft
- Diplomacy
- Welcoming Stance Allies can share your square.
- Advanced Stance Starting at Level 5, one ally gains a bonus on Reflex saves equal to half your level when sharing your square.

### Physician

To be a physician, you must be exposed to the natural world at its least beautiful in your pursuit of knowledge. You learn how creatures’ interior parts work, and need to be able to apply your knowledge in real time without any hesitation.

Starting HP: 10

Ability Score Boost to Dexterity or Wisdom.

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Heal
- Lore
- Profession
- Precise Stance You deal an extra 1d6 points of damage, known as sneak attack damage, when flanking a target or if the target has one of the below conditions.Unconscious Entangled Paralyzed Blinded

### Soldier

To be a soldier, you must serve your community and learn the basics of formal combat. Those called to live this as a lifestyle are suited in some assortment of protective gear, and it becomes their everyday garb. Their mind and practical skills may be limited, but their body is in excellent condition.

Starting HP: 12

Ability Score Boost to Constitution or Strength.

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Armor
- Shields
- Menacing Stance You gain advantage on trained combat skills.
- Advanced Stance Starting at level 5, you gain advantage on all combat skills.

### Tinkerer

To be a tinkerer means that you are the obsessive, never still, producer of gadgets and destroyer of normalcy. Your pursuit of the novel and your process-driven mind compel you toward practical knowledge and mastery of peculiar subjects. In this pursuit, either your mind is sharp enough to avoid causing shrapnel, or you’re fast enough to avoid being hit by it.

Starting HP: 10

Ability Score Boost to Intelligence or Dexterity.

Ability Score Boost to any other Ability Score.

Proficiencies
- Craft
- Disable Device
- Lore
- Shifting Stance You can't be flanked.
- Advanced Stance If an attack misses you, it provokes a reaction.
