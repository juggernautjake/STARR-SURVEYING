## What is it?
**Starr Field** is the Starr Surveying phone app. You'll use it to **clock in and
out**, **snap receipts**, **see your jobs**, **capture field photos/data**, and
**get alerts** from the office — all from your phone.

## Step 3 — Allow the permissions it asks for
The first time, the app asks for a few things. Here's why:
- **Location** — only while you're **clocked in**, so timesheets + mileage are
  accurate and the office knows where the crew is. It **stops within a minute of
  clock-out.**
- **Camera / Photos** — to snap receipts and jobsite/monument photos.
- **Notifications** — so you get job and office alerts.

Tap **Allow** for each — the app needs them to do its job in the field.

---

## Step 4 — Your daily basics
- **Time tab** → tap **Clock In** when you start, **Clock Out** when you're done.
  (Forgot to clock out? You can fix your hours later — they go to the office for
  approval.)
- **Money tab** → tap **+** to photograph a receipt; the app reads it
  automatically. Submit it and the office approves it.
- **Jobs tab** → see the jobs you're assigned to, with details, points, and
  photos.
- **Me tab** → your profile, settings, and sign-out.
