## 1. Goal

Build **Donata Dime** as a real, editable character in the Neon Odyssey campaign on the `/dnd`
platform, with a **fully bespoke character sheet** — a look and a set of on-sheet panels/diagrams
unlike any other character in the campaign — while keeping the standard D&D mechanics underneath.
Sarah (owner) and Andrew (DM) can both edit the sheet, exactly like the other party members.

Her whole identity is a satirical **multi-level-marketing (MLM)** grift: she's a Cleric whose
"deity" is the founder of a pyramid scheme, and her class features are reskinned MLM mechanics.
Her signature twist: **she levels up by climbing the company, not by fighting** — yet she's been
stuck at the bottom rank for six years and has never once lost faith.

Two deliverables:
1. **The interactive artifact** (already shipped) — the flashy pitch-deck / character showpiece.
2. **The in-platform bespoke sheet** (this build) — playable, editable, in Neon Odyssey.

## 3. The company — "Mighty Mojo's Mighty Magic Maguffins"

- **Founder-prophet:** **Mighty Mojo** — a charismatic male guru at the apex of the pyramid. Sells
  "The Method™" (a 12-crystal audio course on abundance mindset). Everyone's buy-in flows up to him.
  He is Donata's literal object of worship (her cleric "deity").
- **"Maguffins" = the reps.** Members proudly call themselves Maguffins ("Become a Maguffin!").
  The double meaning: a *MacGuffin* is a thing everyone chases that turns out not to matter — the
  company means the members; the dictionary means the whole enterprise. Donata is not in on the joke.
- **The Product Line™** (what Maguffins sell — vague, glittery, scammy, Harmonyte-flavored):
  - **Mojo Dust™** — "activated" Harmonyte glitter (luck/energy). It's glitter.
  - **Spark Serum™** — roll-on "vibration" oil, "10× your inner Spark." An oil.
  - **Harmony Wraps™** — contouring body wraps ("that crazy wrap thing"). It's dehydration.
  - **Mojo Shake™** — meal-replacement, "gut harmony in a scoop." Chalk, basically.
  - **Lucky Luminites™** — collectible Harmonyte charms (gotta collect all 214). Literal MacGuffins.
  - **Founder's Bundle (999 gp)** — the buy-in.
- **The scam:** real money comes from **recruiting**, not selling. Monthly minimums, inventory
  loading, "it's not a pyramid, it's a Harmonyte Lattice™ / Sacred Triangle of Abundance™,"
  99.7% of Maguffins lose money, income disclosure in 2-point font.
- **Brand identity:** iridescent holographic *girlboss-glam × prehistoric-saurian × corporate-flash*.
  Palette: hot Mojo magenta `#e0148c`, electric violet `#8b3ff0`, Harmonyte cyan `#0bb7c9`,
  Diamond gold `#d99a1a`, on aubergine/pearlescent grounds. Alliterative, sparkly, cheesy-luxe.

## 5. The signature mechanic — **Rank = Level**

Donata gains **no XP from combat**. She levels **only by climbing the company**: hit a rank's
objectives (recruits / personal volume / team volume / stay "Active") → the DM grants a promotion →
she levels up and gains that level's cleric features. This is diegetic leveling.

The full ladder (level → rank → promotion objective → the "raise" → cleric unlock) is authored in
the artifact and mirrored on-sheet as the **Career Ladder** panel. **She is stuck at Level 3
("Mojo Maker") after six years** — rungs 1–3 achieved, 4–20 aspirational. Her business cards say
"Grand Maguffin Matriarch" (rank 20, self-appointed); the company records say "Mojo Maker."

Ladder summary (full text in the artifact + on-sheet panel):
1 Prospect · 2 Sparkler · **3 Mojo Maker ← she is here (yr 6)** · 4 Radiant Rep · 5 Bronze Boss Babe ·
6 Shimmer Sister · 7 Ruby Recruiter · 8 Sapphire Sponsor · 9 Emerald Executive · 10 Diamond Downline ·
11 Double Diamond · 12 Platinum Priestess · 13 Crown Crystal · 14 Harmonyte Highness ·
15 Presidential Prism · 16 Ambassador of Abundance · 17 Her Serene Diamond Excellency ·
18 Galactic Grandmaster · 19 Legacy Luminary · 20 Grand Maguffin Matriarch.

## 7. Technical architecture (sheet engine)

Files: `app/dnd/_sheet/{types.ts, registry.ts, App.tsx, theme.css, data/*, components/*}`.
Bespoke reference = the **streamer** skin (`skin-streamer` CSS block + `stream` module + custom
components like `StreamChat`/`InfluenceMeter`). *(Exact type/registry shapes confirmed during the
implementation slices from the architecture map.)*

To add Donata we:
1. **`data/donata.ts`** — `donataDime(name)` returns a valid `Character` object (§4 numbers).
2. **`registry.ts`** — register `sheet_type: 'donata'` → skin id + `modules` (incl. a new `mlm`
   module) + variant config.
3. **`theme.css`** — a `.dnd-sheet.skin-donata { … }` block: the bespoke holographic-MLM look
   (fonts, palette, framing) unique to her sheet.
4. **`components/` (new)** — Donata-only panels gated by the `mlm` module and mounted in `App.tsx`:
   - **Downline tracker** — her recruits / "team volume" (mostly zeros, tragically).
   - **Career Ladder (Rank = Level)** — the leveling diagram, "you are here: Lvl 3, yr 6."
   - **The Pyramid / product shelf** — org chart + the Product Line™.
   These are read-first, lightly interactive, and match the artifact's content.
5. Degrade gracefully if the `mlm` module/skin is absent (fall back to a standard sheet).

## 10. Assumptions, risks, verification

- **No live DB from here.** New account/character/skin take effect when the seed is applied / on next
  demo-campaign load (same pattern as the existing demo cast). Everything is written to degrade
  gracefully if unmigrated. The DM applies the seed; behavior is verified there.
- **Fixed UUIDs** (`…a6`, `…c5`) assume no collision with live rows — confirm before applying.
- **Sarah's password:** default to a simple `quick:sarah` password (documented) or passwordless
  entry in open-access mode; the DM can change it.
- **Subrace call:** Tyrasaur (predator) chosen over Herbasaur from the art's sharp teeth + the
  Dimetrodon flavor + Predatory Instinct fitting an MLM closer. Easy to flip if the table prefers.
- **Balance:** the Abundance Domain is homebrew; numbers mirror official cleric power budgets but are
  DM-tunable (all editable on the sheet).
- **Bespoke panels** are additive and module-gated; they never affect other characters' sheets.
